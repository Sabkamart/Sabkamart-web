How Vendors Can Use

Download the Sabka Mart app, register your shop, and reach more customers effortlessly!

Sabkamart - V1.0 Released on March 10, 2025

1️⃣ Register & Set Up Your Shop – Create your online store in minutes.

2️⃣ List Products & Prices – Scan grocery items in your shop for adding and set prices.

3️⃣ Set up discounts – You can also set up discounts for your customers whenever you want

4️⃣ Start Receiving Orders – Accept, pack, and fulfill orders seamlessly.

5️⃣ Own Delivery – You can deliver your customes yourself for free

6️⃣ Opt for Sabka Mart Delivery – If you prefer not to deliver, Delivery partners will handle it for you.
