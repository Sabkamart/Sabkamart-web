Grow Your Business with Sabka Mart

Sabka Mart empowers local grocery stores, supermarkets, and Kirana shops by bringing them online, allowing them to expand their reach, increase sales, and streamline order management effortlessly.

1.Digitize Your Grocery Store

  • Take your business online with zero technical effort and connect with more customers in your area.

  • Attract new buyers who prefer online shopping but still want to support local stores.

  • Stay ahead of competitors by offering both online and offline shopping experiences.

2. Receive & Manage Orders with Ease

  • Accept online orders seamlessly and process them in real-time through the Sabka Mart Vendor App.

  • Manage order statuses such as Accepted, Packed, Ready for Pickup, or Out for Delivery.

  • Keep track of order history, customer preferences, and performance insights.

3.Offer Flexible Discounts to Attract More Customers

  • Set discounts on the total order value instead of individual products—ensuring a fair and simple pricing model.

  • Create custom promotions to retain customers and boost repeat purchases.

  • No hidden charges—you have full control over the discounts you offer.

4.Delivery & Pickup Options for Maximum Convenience

  • Offer flexible delivery options—deliver orders yourself, allow customers to pick up from the store, or use Sabka Mart’s delivery network.

  • With the Self-Pickup System, customers can order online and collect their items at their convenience, avoiding long queues.

5. Use Sabka Mart's Delivery System – Zero Commission Model!

  • Don’t want to handle deliveries? No problem! Sabka Mart has its own dedicated delivery network.

  • Assign orders to verified delivery boys who ensure fast and reliable deliveries.

  • 100% of the delivery fee goes to the delivery boy—Sabka Mart takes no commission on deliveries!

  • This ensures affordable and fair pricing for both vendors and customers.

6. Minimal Platform Fee – Designed for Small & Medium Businesses

  • Sabka Mart charges a small and affordable platform fee, ensuring a cost-effective solution for vendors.

  • No hidden charges, no high commissions! Keep your profits while enjoying premium services.

7. Transparent Pricing – Keep Online & In-Store Prices the Same

  • Unlike other platforms, Sabka Mart ensures no price inflation—what customers pay online is the same as in-store.

  • Build trust and customer loyalty by maintaining price consistency

8. Instant Notifications & Order Alerts

  • Get real-time alerts on new orders, payments, and order status changes.

  • Never miss an order—manage everything on your smartphone.

9. Secure & Flexible Payment Options

  • Accept payments via Cash, UPI, Debit/Credit Cards, and Online Payments.

  • Get secure transactions with fast payment settlements directly to your account.
