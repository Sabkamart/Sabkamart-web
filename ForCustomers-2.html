Shop Smart with Sabka Mart

Sabka Mart makes grocery shopping convenient, affordable, and reliable, ensuring you get the best products from trusted local stores at fair prices.

1.Shop from Your Trusted Local Stores

  • Order groceries and daily essentials from your favorite neighborhood kirana Stores, marts and supermarkets.

  • Support local businesses while enjoying the convenience of online shopping.

  • Get access to fresh and high-quality products from stores near you.

2. Affordable & Transparent Pricing – No Hidden Costs!

  • Pay the same price as in-store shopping—no inflated rates!

  • No hidden fees or unexpected charges—what you see is what you pay.

  • Find budget-friendly grocery deals without compromising on quality.

3.Fast & Reliable Delivery – Your Way, Your Convenience

  • Get your orders delivered to your doorstep quickly and reliably.

  • Choose self-pickup if you prefer to collect your order from the store at your convenience—no waiting in long billing queues!

  • Experience smooth and hassle-free order fulfillment every time.

4.Flexible Discounts & Offers – Save More on Every Order

  • Enjoy exclusive deals and seasonal discounts on total order value.

  • Benefit from special offers provided by your local stores.

  • No coupon codes needed! Discounts are automatically applied at checkout.

5.Easy Returns & Exchanges – Hassle-Free Shopping

  • Not satisfied with your order? Request a return or exchange within 24 hours of delivery.

  • Quickly report damaged, expired, or incorrect items through the app.

  • Get a smooth refund or replacement process without complications.

6. Multiple Payment Options – Pay Your Way

  • Choose from multiple payment methods to suit your preference.

  • Pay using Cash on Delivery (COD), UPI, Debit/Credit Cards, or Net Banking.

  • Enjoy secure and fast transactions with trusted payment gateways.

7.Real-Time Order Updates – Stay Informed

  • Get instant notifications on your order status—Accepted, Packed, Out for Delivery, or Ready for Pickup.

  • Track your order progress and estimated delivery time within the app.

  • Receive alerts for discounts, promotions, and exclusive deals.
