Questions? Look Here.

Some questions about Sabka Mart are frequently asked. We’ve answered the most common ones below

  • How does Sabka Mart work?

    Sabka Mart connects customers, vendors, and delivery partners in one transparent and efficient marketplace.

    • Customers place orders from their favorite local marts.

    • Vendors fulfill orders by delivering them or assigning them to a delivery partner.

    • Delivery partners earn 100% of the delivery fee, making it a fair and profitable model.

  • Does Sabka Mart sell grocery products?

    No! Sabka Mart only provides the platform—all products are listed and sold by shop owners (vendors), ensuring local businesses thrive.

  • Are the prices the same for online and offline shopping?

    Yes! If you visit the shop physically or order online, the prices remain the same, ensuring transparency and fairness for all customers.

  • What delivery options are available?

    Customers can choose between:

    ✅ Home Delivery – Delivered by vendors or Sabka Mart delivery partners.

    ✅ Self-Pickup – Order online & pick up from the store without waiting.

  • How do delivery partners earn?

    The entire delivery amount paid by customers goes directly to the delivery boys. Sabka Mart does not take any commission from this. Our delivery charges are minimal and affordable to make online shopping accessible for everyone.
