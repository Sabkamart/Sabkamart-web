Delivery System

  • Bringing Your Store Closer to Your Customers

At Sabka Mart, we ensure a smooth and efficient delivery experience, making it easy for you to receive your orders at your convenience. Our delivery system is designed to be fast, reliable, and hassle-free.

How Our Delivery System Works
1.Place Your Order

    • Browse through the available shops and add items to your cart.

    • Select your preferred shop and proceed to checkout.

    • Confirm your delivery address and complete the payment.

    2.Order Processing by the Shop

      • The selected shop receives your order and starts preparing it.

      • You will get real-time updates about your order status (Accepted, Rejected, Packed, Out for Delivery, Delivered).

      3.Assigning a Delivery Boy or Vendor Delivery

        • Once your order is ready, it is either assigned to the nearest available delivery boy or delivered directly by the vendor for free.

        • Our system ensures efficient delivery assignment based on proximity and availability.

        4.Fast & Secure Delivery

          • The assigned delivery boy or vendor picks up your order from the shop.

          • Orders are delivered promptly, ensuring fresh and high-quality products.

          • You can track the delivery status within the app.

          5.Order Completion

            • Once delivered, you will receive a confirmation notification.

            • You can rate the delivery experience and provide feedback.

            Key Features of Sabka Mart Delivery

              • ✔Quick & Efficient – Orders are delivered within a short time frame based on shop location.

              • ✔Vendor Delivery Available – Some vendors offer free delivery to customers.

              • ✔ Real-Time Updates – Get live notifications about your order status.

              • ✔ Reliable Delivery Partners – Trained delivery personnel ensure secure and timely delivery.

              • ✔ Flexible Delivery Options – Order from anywhere and get your items delivered at your doorstep.

              • ✔ Self-Pickup Option Available – If you don't want to spend money on delivery, you can pick up your order directly from the shop.

              • 📢 Note: Delivery times may vary depending on shop preparation time and order volume.
